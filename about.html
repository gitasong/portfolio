About Nicole

Brief History

Nicole has an Associate's degree in Computer Graphic Design and a Master's degree in Traditional Chinese Medicine (acupuncture and herbology). After a multi-year illness, she is transitioning to web development to better fit her skill set, evolving interests, and scheduling needs.

Background and Education

Education and Licensure

Nicole holds an M.S.T.O.M (Master's of Science in Traditional Oriental Medicine) from Pacific College of Oriental Medicine, Chicago, and an A.A.S in Computer Graphic Design from the International Academy of Merchandising and Design (now International Acadeny of Design and Technology) in Chicago. She studied religion (Buddhism), music, women's studies, and creative writing at Amherst College, Amherst, MA, and religious studies (Buddhism) at the Naropa Institute (now Naropa University) in Boulder, CO. In addition, she was a creative writing major and voice minor at the Interlochen Arts Academy and the National Music Camp at Interlochen as a high schooler, and is a graduate of the University of Chicago Laboratory Schools High School. She has previously been licensed in acupuncture in Illinois and was certified in Traditional Oriental Medicine (acupuncture and Chinese herbology) by the National Certification Commission for Acupuncture and Oriental Medicine (NCCAOM).
